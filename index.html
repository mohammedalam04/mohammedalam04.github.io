// What we do

Services, delivered with precision

Every service listed below is scoped by a senior engineer, priced as a fixed-fee engagement or managed subscription, and executed end-to-end by Redact Labs — from initial audit to live-event teardown.

01 / CYBERSECURITY

Cybersecurity

Threat protection, vulnerability assessments, firewall hardening, endpoint security, and ongoing monitoring. Aligned to NIST CSF 2.0 and CIS Controls.

Managed from $750/mo · Starts with a posture audit
02 / SECURITY AUDITING

Security Auditing

Comprehensive personal and organisational security audits — identifying exposure points across systems, accounts, and physical environments before attackers do.

Fixed-fee · 2–4 week engagement
03 / IT CONSULTATION

IT Consultation

Strategic technology planning and advisory for businesses of all sizes. Right-sized infrastructure and security decisions from day one.

Hourly, retainer, or project-based
04 / NETWORKING

Networking

Design, installation, and maintenance of wired and wireless networks. Small office setups through to large-scale event infrastructure.

Ubiquiti, Meraki, Aruba certified
05 / EVENT IT

Event IT — Setup & Takedown

Full-service IT deployment and decommissioning for conferences, expos, concerts, and large-scale events. Setup, live support, and clean teardown.

Experienced: MLSE, Live Nation, Toronto venues
06 / AV PANELS

A/V Panel Setup & Programming

Professional LED panel arrays, display systems, and control programming for events and permanent installs. Precision rigging, signal routing, show-ready calibration.

Pixel-perfect calibration · Show-day ready
07 / SERVICE DESK

Service Desk

Responsive IT helpdesk support for day-to-day technical issues — remote or on-site. Ticket-based or retainer models for businesses needing ongoing coverage.

4-hr response SLA available

Personal Security

// Industries

Who we serve

Three sector specialisations where our work is most concentrated.

SPORTS & ENTERTAINMENT

Venues, Franchises, Tours

Event-day AV, temporary and permanent IT infrastructure, multi-venue network deployment, and backstage cyber hardening. Clients include major Toronto franchises and international touring productions.

CHARITIES & NONPROFITS

Charity Cybersecurity Practice

A dedicated five-tier practice built for Canadian charities: Posture Snapshot, Tier 0 Stabilisation, Essentials, Secure, and Guardian. Donor-data protection, PIPEDA / Law 25 / PHIPA guidance, and board-ready reporting.

SMB

Small & Mid-Market Business

Growing businesses (10–200 staff) that have outgrown ad-hoc IT but can't justify a full internal cyber team. Managed security, help desk, and strategic advisory — scaled to fit.

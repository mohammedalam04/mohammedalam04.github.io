What to include in your first message

The more you can share up front, the faster we can get to a useful conversation:

  • Who you are — organisation name (or "individual"), your role
  • What you're looking for — "general scoping call", "security audit quote", "event IT for a specific date", "managed service pricing"
  • Size and context — approximate user / attendee count, industry, timeline
  • Any hard constraints — budget ceiling, regulatory requirements, event dates

If you'd rather just have a conversation, that's completely fine too — a one-line "can we talk?" is enough to get started.

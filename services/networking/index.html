Networking

Redact Labs designs and installs wired and wireless networks for Canadian businesses and events — from a 10-person office to a multi-venue touring production. Every deployment is documented, labelled, and handed over with as-built diagrams.

Networking at Redact Labs covers the physical and logical layer: cabling, switches, routers, firewalls, access points, and VLANs. We design, deploy, document, and optionally manage the network on an ongoing basis. Our engineers are certified on Ubiquiti, Cisco Meraki, and Aruba platforms.

What we deploy

  • Office networks: structured cabling, managed switches, enterprise WiFi, segmented VLANs for guest / IoT / corporate traffic, perimeter firewall
  • Event networks: temporary high-density WiFi, production LAN, uplink redundancy (bonded cellular + fibre), show-critical segmentation
  • Remote-access infrastructure: site-to-site VPN, zero-trust network access (ZTNA), secure remote desktop gateways
  • Venue buildouts: stadium / arena / conference-centre connectivity, distributed WiFi arrays, show-control networks

Platforms we're certified on

  • Ubiquiti UniFi — strong fit for SMB and event deployments; cost-effective and centrally manageable
  • Cisco Meraki — enterprise-grade cloud-managed networking for organisations that need strict SLAs and advanced analytics
  • Aruba Networks — high-density WiFi deployments for venues and stadiums
  • Fortinet and Sophos — perimeter firewalls for organisations with compliance-driven requirements

What's delivered

  • Network design document (logical topology, IP addressing plan, VLAN scheme)
  • As-built diagrams after installation
  • Labelled physical installation (cables, ports, racks)
  • Admin credentials handover in a password manager
  • 30-day post-deployment support included
  • Optional ongoing management as part of a Service Desk retainer

IT Consultation

Redact Labs provides strategic IT consultation for Canadian businesses making significant technology decisions — infrastructure buildouts, platform migrations, security architecture, and vendor selection. We help you make the right call before you sign the contract.

IT consultation at Redact Labs is vendor-agnostic advisory work. We don't resell hardware or software; we review your options, pressure-test your assumptions, and give you a written recommendation with clear reasoning. Most engagements are 10–40 hours over 4–8 weeks.

What we're commonly asked to help with

  • Microsoft 365 vs Google Workspace — which platform fits, licensing tier to choose, migration planning
  • Donor CRM / business platform selection — comparing Little Green Light, Keela, Sumac, Salesforce NPSP, Bloomerang, DonorPerfect, and others
  • Network infrastructure design — for a new office build, venue fit-out, or remote-first transition
  • Cyber insurance readiness — what controls your underwriter will actually require
  • Compliance roadmap — what CyberSecure Canada, SOC 2, or ISO 27001 actually requires to achieve
  • Board and funder reporting — turning technical posture into board-digestible language

How we work

Most consulting engagements follow a three-phase structure:

  1. Discovery (week 1). Interviews with stakeholders, review of existing documentation, clarification of success criteria.
  2. Analysis (weeks 2–3). Research, option scoring, vendor conversations on your behalf, cost modelling.
  3. Recommendation (week 4). Written recommendation with reasoning, costs, risks, and implementation path. Optional readout meeting.

Pricing

  • Hourly: $200/hr for ad-hoc advice
  • Retainer: from $1,500/month for ongoing strategic partnership
  • Project-based: fixed fee, scoped up front, typically $3,500–$15,000

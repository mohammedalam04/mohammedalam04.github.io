Event IT — Setup & Takedown

Redact Labs delivers end-to-end IT for live events — conferences, concerts, expos, and touring productions. We spin up the entire IT stack (network, AV infrastructure, show control) in 24–72 hours, provide on-site live support through the event, and tear it down cleanly.

Event IT at Redact Labs is a complete temporary-infrastructure service for live productions. It covers the network (high-density WiFi, production LAN, internet uplinks), the AV infrastructure (signal routing, display systems, recording), and on-site show-time support. We've delivered event IT for major Toronto sports franchises, international touring productions, and premium venues.

What we deliver at an event

  • Network: bonded internet uplinks (fibre + multi-carrier cellular), high-density WiFi for attendees, segregated production LAN, VLANs for payment / BOH / production
  • AV infrastructure: signal routing, LED panel arrays, display systems, recording infrastructure (see AV Panels)
  • Registration and ticketing IT: POS hardening, scanner infrastructure, offline failover
  • Backstage / production IT: timing systems, show control networks, radio and comms integration
  • Live support: on-site engineer throughout the event, dedicated Slack / phone line for show staff
  • Clean teardown: everything labelled, inventoried, and returned. No cables left in the venue.

Experience

Redact Labs has delivered event IT for sports and entertainment clients including:

  • Major Toronto sports franchises and MLSE-associated venues
  • Live Nation touring productions
  • Premium Toronto event venues
  • Multi-city Canadian and US tour dates, including international touring acts

How we scope an event engagement

Event engagements are quoted per event based on:

  • Event size (attendee count, venue footprint)
  • Infrastructure complexity (WiFi density, AV signal routing, show-control needs)
  • Crew size and days on-site
  • Equipment rental vs. client-provided
  • Travel and accommodation (for multi-city tours)

Expect a written quote within 48 hours of an initial scoping call. Most events are fixed-fee with clearly-defined change-order triggers.

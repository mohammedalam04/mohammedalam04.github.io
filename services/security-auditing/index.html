Security Auditing

A Redact Labs security audit is a fixed-fee, 2–4 week engagement that produces a written, board-ready report identifying the most significant security exposures across your systems, accounts, and physical environment — with a specific remediation plan and indicative costs.

A security audit from Redact Labs is a point-in-time assessment combining structured interviews, a non-intrusive external scan, and a review of your identity and access, endpoint, email, and data-protection controls. It is not a penetration test and not a compliance certification — it is a diagnostic report that tells you what to fix first and why.

What's in an audit

Every audit includes

  • 90-minute structured interview with leadership and key operational staff
  • 30-point external scan of public-facing infrastructure (DNS, email authentication, website TLS, exposed services, breach-database checks)
  • Identity and access review — who has access to what, including dormant accounts
  • NIST CSF 2.0 current-state profile across all six functions
  • CIS IG1 gap analysis
  • Risk heat-map — impact × likelihood for every finding
  • Top 10 ranked findings with plain-English explanations
  • 30/60/90-day remediation plan with indicative costs
  • Written report, 8–12 pages, delivered within 5 business days of the kickoff interview
  • Optional 30-minute readout meeting with leadership or the board

Who the audit is for

Security audits are the appropriate first step for any organisation that:

  • Has never had a formal cybersecurity assessment
  • Is renewing or applying for cyber insurance and needs a baseline
  • Is responding to funder, client, or regulatory requirements
  • Is considering a managed cybersecurity service and wants to know what they're buying
  • Has just experienced a security incident and needs a post-incident baseline

What the audit is not

A Redact Labs audit is deliberately scoped to be useful, fast, and affordable. It is not:

  • A penetration test (we don't attempt to exploit vulnerabilities)
  • A regulatory compliance certification (we help you prepare, not certify)
  • A forensic investigation (that's a separate engagement)
  • A long-form consulting report you'll never read

Pricing

Audits are fixed-fee based on organisation size:

  • Individual / sole proprietor: from $1,500
  • Small organisation (up to 15 users): from $2,500
  • Mid-market (15–75 users): from $5,000
  • Larger organisations (75+ users): scoped on request

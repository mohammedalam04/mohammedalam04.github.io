Service Desk

Redact Labs' Service Desk is a responsive IT helpdesk for businesses that need consistent day-to-day technical support without the overhead of a full internal IT team. Remote and on-site options, with retainer plans sized to your staff count.

The Service Desk at Redact Labs is a tiered IT support offering: ticket-based for smaller clients (pay-as-you-go or low-hours retainer), or full managed-helpdesk retainer for organisations that need predictable coverage. Response SLAs range from next-business-day to 4-hour depending on tier.

Two ways to engage

Ticket-based (pay-as-you-go)

For organisations with light, occasional support needs. Submit a ticket, we respond within one business day, bill against your prepaid hours block. Hours don't expire. Suitable for organisations with fewer than 15 staff or for bridging while evaluating a full retainer.

Managed helpdesk retainer

For organisations needing predictable coverage. Monthly fixed fee based on staff count. Includes:

  • Unlimited user-support tickets
  • 4-hour response SLA on urgent; same-business-day on standard
  • Endpoint monitoring and patch management
  • Quarterly service review
  • After-hours emergency coverage (add-on)

What's in scope

  • User account issues (password resets, access requests, MFA setup)
  • Laptop and workstation support (setup, troubleshooting, software install)
  • Email, Microsoft 365, and Google Workspace support
  • Printer, phone, and peripheral issues
  • Basic network troubleshooting
  • Security incident triage and escalation
  • New-hire onboarding and offboarding

Pricing

  • Ticket block: from $150/hour, 10-hour blocks
  • Managed retainer: from $45/user/month (minimum 10 users)
  • After-hours emergency coverage: from $500/month add-on

Personal Security

Redact Labs' personal security service is a tailored cyber programme for individuals who need more than consumer-grade protection — executives, public figures, high-net-worth individuals, and anyone whose digital safety has real-world consequences.

Personal security at Redact Labs covers the full stack of an individual's digital life: account hardening across banking, email, social, and cloud; device security (laptop, phone, tablet, home network); privacy configuration; digital footprint review; and ongoing monitoring for credential leaks and impersonation.

What's included

Personal Security Baseline

  • Account audit — every significant account identified, password-manager migration, MFA enabled on everything that supports it
  • Device hardening — laptop, phone, tablet, and home network configured against common attack patterns
  • Email security — forwarding rules audited, sign-in monitoring configured, legacy accounts retired
  • Privacy configuration — data-broker opt-outs, social media privacy review, public-records review
  • Digital footprint review — what's publicly visible about you and your family, what can be removed, what to be aware of
  • Breach monitoring — ongoing alerts if your credentials appear in new data breaches
  • Incident response plan — a one-page plan for "what to do if" (phone stolen, account compromised, impersonation attempt)

Who we work with

  • Executives and founders whose company compromise could flow through their personal accounts
  • Public figures — politicians, journalists, content creators, professional athletes
  • High-net-worth individuals concerned about targeted attacks
  • Family offices coordinating protection across household members

How we work

Every personal engagement is discreet. We sign an NDA before the first meeting. All communication is via end-to-end-encrypted channels. Nothing is shared beyond the Redact Labs senior engineer assigned to the client.

A typical engagement:

  1. Initial scoping call (1 hour, encrypted video)
  2. Full audit (4–8 hours over 1–2 weeks)
  3. Baseline implementation (2–3 sessions, remote or in-person)
  4. Ongoing monitoring (optional, monthly retainer)

Pricing

  • Personal security baseline: from $2,500 one-time
  • Family package (up to 4 household members): from $6,000 one-time
  • Ongoing monitoring retainer: from $300/month

A/V Panel Setup & Programming

Redact Labs delivers professional LED panel arrays, display systems, and control programming for live events and permanent installations. Precision rigging, clean signal routing, and pixel-perfect calibration — show-ready every time.

AV panel setup at Redact Labs is the complete delivery of a display system — from the physical rigging of LED panels, through signal routing and processor programming, to final pixel-perfect calibration. We work with industry-standard processors (Novastar, Brompton, Colorlight) and rigging from major vendors.

What we deliver

  • LED panel arrays: wall-sized displays, curved setups, ground-stacked and flown configurations
  • Display systems: conventional LCD / OLED arrays for conference and corporate events
  • Signal routing: SDI, HDMI, NDI, and IP-based video distribution
  • Control programming: Novastar, Brompton, Colorlight, and ROE processor configuration
  • Pixel mapping: content-to-canvas mapping for irregular or multi-surface deployments
  • Calibration: colour matching, brightness uniformity, refresh-rate tuning for camera-visible displays
  • Rigging: truss, motor, and structural integration

Who we serve

  • Live concerts and tours — including multi-city Canadian and US tours
  • Corporate events — keynotes, product launches, conferences
  • Sports and entertainment venues — permanent and temporary installations
  • Houses of worship — live-stream and congregation displays
  • Retail and brand activations — pop-ups, showrooms, trade-show booths

Current engagements

Redact Labs is currently delivering multi-city AV panel programming for touring productions across Canadian and US venues, including major Nasheed artist tours spanning Toronto, Mississauga, Ottawa, Montreal, Houston, and New York.

Pricing

AV engagements are quoted per event or per installation. For live events, a typical scope covers:

  • Pre-event design and programming (1–2 days)
  • Load-in and setup (1 day)
  • Show-day operation and on-call support
  • Strike and teardown

Expect a detailed written quote within 48 hours of the scoping call.
